How to Play Ludo:

  • Roll the Dice: Tap the dice to roll and move your tokens based on the number you get.
  • Strategize: Move your token wisely to block opponents. Utilize strategic moves to gain an advantage and pave your path to victory.
  • Capture Tokens: Land on an opponent's token to send it back to the start. Capturing tokens is key to winning.
  • Reach Home: Get all your tokens to the center of the board using various strategies to win the game.
